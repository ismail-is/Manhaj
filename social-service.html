Community Programs

Social Service

Empowering families across Mangaluru through education aid, medical support, ration drives, and Eid programs.

Program 01

Education Support

Our Education Support program provides scholarships, school bags, uniforms, and stationery to underprivileged students in Mangaluru. Every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their family's financial situation.

Families can apply for support through our contact form. Donors can contribute to sponsor a student's educational needs for an entire academic year.

  • Scholarships
  • School bags, uniforms & stationery
  • Annual student sponsorship
Apply for Support Donate
Program 02

Medical Aid & Support

We assist community members facing medical emergencies with hospital bill support, medication costs, and connecting them with local clinic partnerships.

Those in need can apply through our contact form to receive timely assistance during their time of need.

  • Hospital bill support
  • Medication support
  • Local clinic partnerships
Apply for Aid Donate
Program 03

Family Ration Distribution

Monthly ration distribution drives providing essential food supplies to needy families in Mangaluru. Each kit contains rice, dal, cooking oil, sugar, tea, and other staple items sufficient for a month's needs.

Donors can sponsor a family's monthly ration through a fixed contribution, making a tangible difference every month.

  • Monthly drives
  • Essential food items
  • Fixed contribution per family
Sponsor a Family's Ration
Program 04

Eid Kiswa

Before every Eid, we distribute new clothing to families who cannot afford new garments for the celebration. Our Eid Kiswa program sources quality clothing locally and ensures families celebrate Eid with dignity.

Because every Muslim deserves to experience the joy of Eid � regardless of their circumstances.

  • Before every Eid
  • New clothing distribution
  • Locally sourced quality garments
Sponsor Eid Clothing
Program 05

Eid Kit

Comprehensive Eid Kit packages containing new clothing, sweets, food items, and Eid essentials for families who cannot afford celebrations. Ensuring every family experiences the joy of Eid.

  • New clothing
  • Sweets & treats
  • Food items
  • Eid essentials
