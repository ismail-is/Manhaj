Da'wah Programs

Spreading authentic Islamic knowledge through classes, workshops, media, and community outreach.

Program 01

Tasfiyah & Tarbiyah (Islamic Classes)

Regular Islamic classes focusing on Tasfiyah (purification of creed and practice) and Tarbiyah (cultivation of character). Weekly/bi-weekly sessions covering essential aspects of correct Islamic belief and practice.

Following a lecture and Q&A format, with separate sittings for brothers and sisters. Topics include Tawheed, Aqeedah, Seerah, daily etiquettes, and contemporary issues.

  • Weekly / Bi-weekly schedule
  • Separate sittings for brothers & sisters
  • Lecture + Q&A format

Program 02

Islamic Workshops

Intensive one or two-day workshops on specific Islamic topics including marriage in Islam, parenting according to the Sunnah, business ethics, and contemporary issues.

These workshops bring together specialists and community members for focused learning. Registration is required for all workshops.

  • 1�2 day intensive format
  • Registration required

Program 03

Multi Language Islamic Library

Our Islamic library houses a diverse collection of books in Kannada, Arabic, English, Urdu, and Tamil, covering Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, Seerah, and contemporary Islamic thought.

The library operates on a borrowing system with designated open days for the community to visit, browse, and borrow books.

  • 5 Languages available
  • Borrowing system available

Program 04

Multi Media Wing / Islamic Studio

Our Islamic studio produces audio and video content for da'wah purposes, distributing through YouTube, social media, and our web portal.

The studio enables us to reach a wider audience with authentic Islamic content, serving both local and global communities.

  • Audio & Video production
  • YouTube & Social Media distribution
Program 05

Web Portal & Online Islamic Radio
