Our Programs

Education at Manhaj Al Ambiya

Structured Islamic education programs for all ages — rooted in the Quran and authentic Sunnah.

Program 01

Arabic Academy

Our Arabic Academy offers structured courses from beginner to advanced levels, focusing on Nahw, Sarf, and reading comprehension. Designed for adult learners who wish to understand the Quran in its original language.

Students progress through carefully designed levels using time-tested methodologies combined with modern teaching aids.

  • Beginner to Advanced levels
  • Nahw, Sarf, and Reading Comprehension
  • Designed for adult learners
  • Separate classes for brothers & sisters
Enroll Now
Program 02

Quran Tajweed & Hifz

Our Quran program offers both Tajweed (proper recitation rules) and Hifz (memorisation) tracks. Students master the articulation points of each letter and the rules governing their recitation.

The Hifz track provides structured memorisation with an accountability system. Students who complete the program may pursue Ijazah.

  • Tajweed rules & proper recitation
  • Structured Hifz memorisation track
  • Accountability system
  • Ijazah pathway available
Enroll Now
Program 03

Madrasah & Islamic Studies

Comprehensive Islamic education covering Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, Hadith studies, and Tafsir. Designed for children and young adults with a structured term-based curriculum.

Students develop a strong understanding of Islamic principles and their practical application in daily life.

  • Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, Hadith, Tafsir
  • Children and young adults
  • Term-based structured curriculum
Enroll Now
Program 04

Teen Series (Saturday · Girls)

Specifically designed for Muslim girls aged 13–18. Addresses topics crucial to teenage Muslim identity, sisters' fiqh, and building a meaningful connection with the Quran.

Sessions are interactive and discussion-based, creating a safe space for young sisters to learn, question, and grow in their deen.

  • Ages 13–18 (Girls only)
  • Identity, sisters' fiqh, Quran connection
  • Interactive & discussion-based format
Enroll Now
Program 05

Islamic Pre School

A nurturing play-based learning environment for children aged 3–6, introducing Arabic alphabets, essential duas, basic aqeedah, and Islamic manners.

Building a strong Islamic foundation from the earliest years, helping children develop a love for their deen alongside general early education.

  • Ages 3–6
  • Play-based Islamic learning
  • Arabic alphabets, duas, basic aqeedah
