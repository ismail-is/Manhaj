Who We Are

Manhaj-Al-Ambiya, Mangaluru

The Manhaj-Al-Ambiya, Mangaluru is a non-profit Islamic education and da'wah group with a registered trust. The primary objective is to adhere to the methodology of the pious predecessors with the correct understanding of Quran & Sunnah.

Our trust is committed to the propagation of authentic Islamic knowledge, based on the Quran and Sunnah as understood by the Salaf as-Salih (pious predecessors). We operate educational programs, published Islamic literature, organise da'wah activities, and provide social services to the Muslim community and the broader public in Mangaluru and surrounding areas.

We believe in the importance of tasfiyah (purification) and tarbiyah (cultivation) — purifying Islam from shirk, bid'ah, and misconceptions while cultivating individuals upon the correct understanding.

"Our primary goal is to teach the correct Islam in our city and local area to clear up the misconceptions of both Muslims and non-Muslims."

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our trust, programs, and how to get involved.
  • What is Manhaj Al Ambiya?
    Manhaj Al Ambiya is a registered non-profit Islamic education and da'wah trust based in Mangaluru, Karnataka. We are dedicated to spreading authentic Islamic knowledge based on the Quran and Sunnah as understood by the Salaf as-Salih (pious predecessors).
  • What programs and courses do you offer?
    We offer a wide range of programs including Quranic Tajweed, Hifz (memorisation), Arabic language studies, Islamic Fiqh, and da'wah training. Our Madrasah caters to both children and adults, with separate classes for male and female students.
  • How can I enrol my child or myself?
    You can enrol by visiting our office at First Floor, Majestic Tower, Pandeshwar, Mangaluru, or by contacting us directly via phone (+91 96110 71429) or email (manhajmangalore@gmail.com). Our team will guide you through the admission process.
  • Is the trust registered and transparent?
    Yes. Manhaj Al Ambiya is a fully registered non-profit Islamic trust operating under authentic prophetic methodology. We operate with full transparency and community accountability. All donations are used exclusively for education, da'wah, and community welfare activities.
  • How can I support or donate to your cause?
    You can donate through our UPI QR code available on our homepage, or contact us directly for bank transfer details. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps us sustain our educational programs and serve the community.
